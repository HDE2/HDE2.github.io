About Me
Technical Specialist | Audio Engineer | Team Lead

Since childhood, I’ve had an almost alarming tendency to take apart home computers and stereos. Don’t worry—I’ve since learned how to put things back together (and no workplace electronics were harmed in the process). This early obsession with technology turned me into a creative problem-solver who thrives on breaking down complex challenges.

My passion for tech, combined with an undying love for sci-fi, music, and gaming, has fueled a career that spans from mixing live audio in stadiums to producing original content. No matter the medium, I bring a mix of technical expertise, lateral thinking, and a dash of unconventional problem-solving.

Fueled by curiosity and caffeine, I stay motivated, meet deadlines, and adapt to any challenge thrown my way. When you love what you do, staying dedicated isn’t a chore—it’s just how you operate.
